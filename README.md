# Academix App

Welcome to **Academix**, your ultimate companion for academic success! Designed to streamline your study schedule and track your progress, this app combines cutting-edge technology with a sleek, intuitive interface to make learning easier and more enjoyable.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 🎯 **Dashboard**
- Get a bird's-eye view of your academic journey.
- Track key stats like tasks, subjects, and hours completed—all in one place.

### 📚 **Subject Management**
- Easily add, edit, and manage your subjects.
- Keep tabs on hours completed for each subject and stay on track.

### ✅ **Task Management**
- Add tasks with deadlines and assign them to specific subjects.
- Stay productive by marking tasks as complete and monitoring your progress.

### ⏳ **Time Service**
- Log and track your study hours with precision.
- Measure your productivity and ensure no subject gets left behind.

### 🎨 **Modern Material Design**
- Enjoy a visually stunning and highly responsive UI built with Material 3.
- Personalize your experience with adaptive, user-focused design.

---

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

### **Frontend:**
- **Jetpack Compose**: Declarative, efficient, and easy-to-use UI framework.
- **Material 3 Design**: Bringing Material You to life with dynamic theming and a modern aesthetic.

### **Backend:**
- **Room Database**: Fast, reliable local data storage for offline capabilities.
- **Dagger Hilt**: Simplifies dependency injection to keep code clean and modular.
- **Compose Destinations**: Hassle-free navigation with a focus on simplicity and readability.
